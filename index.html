About Us

Welcome to Kit Finder, the ultimate destination for football shirt collectors and enthusiasts around the world.

Our mission is simple: to make finding your dream football shirt as easy as possible. Whether you're searching for a classic vintage jersey from the 90s, a rare limited edition kit, or the latest release from your favourite club, we've got you covered.

Kit Finder is a search engine that aggregates listings from multiple trusted football shirt retailers and marketplaces. We don't sell shirts directly — instead, we help you discover, compare, and find the best deals across the web.

Our platform features thousands of shirts from clubs across the globe, spanning decades of football history. From Premier League giants to Serie A legends, from national teams to obscure lower-league gems — if it exists, you can find it here.

We are passionate collectors ourselves, and we built Kit Finder because we wanted a better way to hunt for shirts. We hope it helps you find that special jersey you've been looking for.

100% Authentic — Zero Fakes

Every single shirt on Kit Finder is 100% original and genuine. We partner exclusively with trusted specialist retailers who sell only authentic vintage and retro football shirts. You will never find a fake, replica or counterfeit shirt through Kit Finder. That's our promise.
