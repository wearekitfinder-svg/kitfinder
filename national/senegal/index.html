The most collectible Senegal kits

The 2002 Korea/Japan World Cup shirt is Senegal's most historic jersey. Making their World Cup debut, the Lions of Teranga — coached by Bruno Metsu — defeated defending champions France in the opening game and went on to reach the quarter-finals. El Hadji Diouf was the star of the tournament. This kit is the most sought-after Senegal shirt ever made.

The 2022 Africa Cup of Nations shirt — Senegal's first AFCON title, won in Cameroon under Aliou Cissé — is the most popular modern Senegal jersey. Sadio Mané, despite missing the final penalty in the shoot-out win over Egypt, led Senegal to their greatest honour. He won the tournament's Golden Ball award.

The 2022 Qatar World Cup shirt — Senegal's third World Cup appearance — saw them reach the round of 16. Despite losing Mané to injury before the tournament, they performed admirably, defeated Ecuador to qualify from their group.

Sadio Mané shirts from his Senegal career span both his Liverpool and Bayern Munich years at club level. His combination of club and international success makes him Senegal's most collectible player by some distance.
