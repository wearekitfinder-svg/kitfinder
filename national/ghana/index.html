The most collectible Ghana kits

The 2006 Germany World Cup shirt — Ghana's debut World Cup appearance in the modern era — is the most collected vintage Ghana jersey. The Black Stars qualified as African champions and performed admirably, defeating the Czech Republic and the United States before bowing out in the round of 16.

The 2010 South Africa World Cup shirt is Ghana's most emotionally significant jersey. The Black Stars reached the quarter-finals — one step from the semi-finals — before Luis Suárez's infamous handball on the goal line and Asamoah Gyan's missed penalty eliminated them against Uruguay. This shirt carries enormous emotional weight for African football fans.

Ghana are the only African nation to have won the FIFA Under-20 World Cup twice (1991 and 2009), producing shirts that are collected by fans of youth football history.

Players like Michael Essien, Asamoah Gyan and Sulley Muntari are Ghana's most iconic modern players. Their shirts from the 2006 and 2010 World Cup eras are the most individually collected Ghana player shirts.

Key facts

  • Ghana have won the Africa Cup of Nations four times — 1963, 1965, 1978 and 1982.
  • The Black Stars were the first African team to reach a World Cup quarter-final in Africa (2010).
  • Ghana's U-20 team are the most successful African nation in the FIFA World Youth Championship.
  • Michael Essien is widely regarded as Ghana's greatest modern player.
