The most collectible Nigeria kits

The 1994 USA World Cup shirt — Nigeria's first World Cup appearance — is the most historically significant Super Eagles jersey. The bold green kit with its distinctive design caused a sensation. Nigeria defeated Bulgaria and Greece to top their group, with Rashidi Yekini's iconic roar after scoring their first ever World Cup goal.

The 1996 Atlanta Olympics shirt is Nigeria's greatest moment. The Dream Team — featuring Jay-Jay Okafor, Nwankwo Kanu and Celestine Babayaro — won Olympic gold, defeating Argentina and Brazil on the way. This Adidas kit is one of the most collectible African football shirts ever made.

The 2018 Russia World Cup shirt by Adidas became one of the most viral football shirts in history. Its bold green and white geometric design caused queues outside Nike stores worldwide and sold out almost immediately. Original retail examples are now collectible in themselves.

Jay-Jay Okafor shirts from his Super Eagles career are the most individually collected Nigeria player pieces. His dazzling skill and eccentric personality made him one of football's most beloved figures.

Key facts

  • Nigeria have won the Africa Cup of Nations three times — 1980, 1994 and 2013.
  • The 1996 Olympic gold medal team is Nigeria's greatest sporting achievement.
  • The 2018 Adidas World Cup shirt sold out globally within minutes of its launch.
  • Jay-Jay Okafor is widely considered Nigeria's greatest ever player.
  • Nigeria have qualified for the World Cup six times, debuting in 1994.
