The most collectible Morocco kits

The 1986 Mexico World Cup shirt is Morocco's most historic jersey. The Atlas Lions became the first African and Arab nation to advance from the group stage of a World Cup, finishing above England, Poland and Portugal. This rare kit is the holy grail for Morocco shirt collectors.

The 2022 Qatar World Cup shirt is Morocco's modern masterpiece. Eliminating Belgium, Spain and Portugal on the way to the semi-finals — the furthest any African or Arab nation has ever gone in a World Cup — made this Puma shirt one of the most talked-about jerseys of the tournament.

Hakim Ziyech's Morocco shirts from the 2022 World Cup are the most individually collected Atlas Lions player pieces. His brilliant left foot and spectacular goals made him one of the tournament's standout players.

The Africa Cup of Nations has brought Morocco further glory, with the Atlas Lions winning in 1976 and consistently reaching the later rounds of the tournament since.

Key facts
