The most collectible Manchester United kits

The 1992-94 Sharp/Umbro home shirt — worn during the first Premier League title — is one of the most collected Manchester United shirts. Its simple, classic design captures the beginning of the Premier League era perfectly.

The 1999 treble season shirt (worn in the Champions League final comeback against Bayern Munich) is the single most sought-after Manchester United jersey. Any shirt from that historic season commands premium prices, with player-name versions fetching considerably more.

The 1995-96 grey away shirt is one of football's most infamous kits — famously substituted at half-time against Southampton because the players claimed they couldn't see each other. That story has made it one of the most iconic away shirts ever made.

Eric Cantona shirts from the early 1990s are among the most desirable player shirts for Man United collectors. His collar-up swagger and iconic status make any Cantona shirt instantly recognisable.

Manchester United kits by decade

  • 1970s-80s: Admiral and then Umbro supplied United. Classic red home shirts from this era are rare and collectible.
  • 1990s: The Sharp-sponsored Umbro era is the golden age for United collectors. The 1999 treble kit is the crown jewel.
  • 2000s: Nike took over in 2002 and supplied through 2015. The Ronaldo era (2003-09) shirts are highly collected.
  • 2010s-present: Adidas supply the kits from 2015. Vintage collectors tend to focus on the Umbro and early Nike eras.
