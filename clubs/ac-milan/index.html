The most collectible AC Milan kits

The 1988-89 and 1989-90 European Cup winning shirts — worn by Gullit, Van Basten and Rijkaard — are the most prized AC Milan collector pieces. These Adidas kits represent the peak of Milan's dominance and are now extremely rare in good condition.

Paolo Maldini spent his entire career at Milan and his shirts from the early 1990s through the 2000s are widely collected. As one of the greatest defenders in history and a one-club man, Maldini shirts carry enormous sentimental and collector value.

The 2002-03 and 2006-07 Champions League winning shirts are popular modern collector pieces. The 2003 final against Juventus (decided on penalties) and the 2007 final revenge over Liverpool are iconic moments.

The Kaka era shirts (2003-09) are highly sought after. His 2006-07 Champions League-winning season is considered one of the greatest individual seasons in football history.

AC Milan kits by decade

  • 1980s: Adidas supplied Milan during the Sacchi era. The 1988-90 European Cup winning kits are the most valuable.
  • 1990s: Lotto took over and produced some elegant kits. The Capello-era Lotto shirts are popular with collectors.
  • 2000s: Opel-sponsored Adidas kits. The Kaka and Shevchenko era produced some of the most collected modern Milan shirts.
  • 2010s-present: Adidas, then Puma. Modern collectors tend to focus on the earlier decades.
