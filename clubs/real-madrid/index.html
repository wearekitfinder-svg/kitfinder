The most collectible Real Madrid kits

The 1980s Hummel era is the golden age for Real Madrid shirt collectors. The Danish brand produced beautifully simple all-white kits with the distinctive Hummel chevrons on the shoulders. Any original Hummel Real Madrid shirt is now a prized collector's item.

The Galacticos era shirts (2001-06) — featuring Zidane, Ronaldo, Beckham, Figo and Raúl — are among the most collected modern football shirts. Adidas produced the kits during this period, and player-name shirts from this era sell for significant sums.

Zinedine Zidane's match-worn and player-issue shirts from his time at Madrid (2001-06) are among the most valuable football shirts in the world. His volley in the 2002 Champions League final is one of football's greatest moments.

The 2015-16 and 2016-17 Champions League winning shirts — the back-to-back European titles under Zidane as manager — are hugely popular with modern collectors.

Real Madrid kits by decade

  • 1970s-80s: Hummel, then Adidas supplied Real Madrid. The Hummel shirts are the most collectible from this era.
  • 1990s: Kelme and then Adidas. The 1998 Champions League winning shirt (Predator era) is a collector favourite.
  • 2000s: The Galacticos era under Adidas produced iconic kits. The 2001-02 centenary kit with the gold trim is particularly striking.
  • 2010s-present: Adidas continue. The three consecutive Champions League winning kits (2016, 2017, 2018) are highly sought after.
