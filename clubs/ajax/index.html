The most collectible Ajax kits

The 1994-95 Champions League winning shirt is the most sought-after Ajax jersey ever made. The young Ajax team — featuring a teenage Patrick Kluivert, Clarence Seedorf, Edgar Davids and coached by Louis van Gaal — beat AC Milan 1-0 in Vienna. This Umbro shirt is now extremely rare in original form.

The Johan Cruyff era shirts from Ajax's three consecutive European Cup winning years (1971, 1972, 1973) are among the rarest and most valuable football shirts in existence. Any authenticated Cruyff-era Ajax shirt is a museum piece.

The early 1990s Ajax kits — especially the 1991-92 Umbro shirt worn by the team that reached the UEFA Cup final — introduced many of the players who would go on to win the 1995 Champions League.

Dennis Bergkamp Ajax shirts from his early career (1986-93) are collected both by Ajax fans and Arsenal fans who followed his later career. His shirts from this era predate his move to Inter and then Arsenal.

Ajax kits by decade

  • 1970s: Adidas and Umbro. The Total Football era kits are the rarest and most valuable Ajax shirts.
  • 1980s: Umbro supplied Ajax through most of this decade, including early Bergkamp and Van Basten years.
  • 1990s: Umbro continued and produced the iconic 1995 Champions League winning kit.
  • 2000s-present: Adidas supply Ajax from 2000. The 2018-19 Champions League semi-final run produced a modern classic.
